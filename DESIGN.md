## Overview

Hearth design systems support the Utility Warehouse multi-product platform. The
visual language is warm, approachable, and purposeful — rooted in a deep brand
purple with warm-white surfaces that feel human rather than sterile.

Six UW service lines; **energy**, **mobile**, **broadband**, **insurance**, and
**cashback**, each carry a distinct colour. These service colours appear
consistently across components, tokens, mascot illustrations, and product
scenes, making product context visually immediate. Every component surface,
shadow, and icon can reflect the service being presented.

The type system pairs a custom display face (Comic Hams) for headings with DM
Sans for body copy and DM Mono for detail contexts. The overall rhythm is
generous: warm-white backgrounds, large border-radius, and soft coloured shadows
create a layered but uncluttered hierarchy.

### Brand & Accent

- **Brand Background** ({colors.background-brand}): The signature Linear accent — primary CTA, brand mark, link emphasis.

- **Brand Secure** ({colors.brand-secure}): Muted lavender-gray (#7a7fad) — used in "Linear Security" surfaces.
